It’s understandable to feel conflicted about charging for a beta test, Sir.  Many people perceive "beta" as free or low-cost due to the inherent experimental nature.

Transparency is key. Clearly communicate the value users get for their contribution – early access, direct influence on development, exclusive features, etc.  Emphasize that their feedback directly shapes the final product. If you can demonstrate the model's strong performance and potential, coupled with a clear roadmap of future improvements, you might alleviate concerns about it being "garbage."
